Site Plan - JUR Music Project

Site Name

Name: JUR Music

Justification: The name "JUR Music" reflects the personal and professional identity of JUR, an emerging musician and rockstar from Querétaro, Mexico. The simplicity and memorability of the name make it easy for fans to remember, and it directly connects to his music and artistic persona. JUR is an independent artist looking to gain recognition and build a fanbase, and this name will help establish that brand presence.

Domain Availability: jur-music.com (available for purchase)

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to serve as an official online hub for JUR, where visitors can explore his music, learn about his influences, and stay up to date with his projects and upcoming events. The website will also provide an easy way for fans to connect with JUR, follow his journey, and engage with his music. As an emerging artist, JUR is looking to expand his reach and share his talent with the world.

Scenarios

  • What are JUR's musical influences?
  • How can I join the JUR movement and support his music?
  • What are the upcoming events or concerts where JUR will perform?
  • Where can I listen to JUR's music or purchase it?

Color Scheme

Primary Color: Black (#000000) - This will be the background color for the site to give it a sleek, modern look.

Secondary Color: White (#FFFFFF) - Used for text throughout the site to provide clear readability against the dark background.

Accent Color: Green (#2ECC71) - This color will be used for call-to-action buttons, links, and some icons to create a vibrant contrast and draw attention to key interactive elements.

Typography

Main Font: Rock Salt (for headers) - This font gives the site a unique, artistic feel that aligns with JUR's rockstar identity.

Secondary Font: Merienda (for body text) - A clean and readable font that complements the main font and is ideal for longer text content.
